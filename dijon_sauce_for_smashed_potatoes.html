Dijon Sauce for Smashed Potatoes

Description: A creamy and tangy Dijon sauce perfect for drizzling over smashed potatoes or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter.
  2. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until the mixture is lightly golden.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce begins to thicken, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the Dijon mustard, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes until fully combined and creamy.
  5. Remove from heat and adjust seasoning if needed. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.

Notes

For a richer flavor, you can substitute half of the milk with heavy cream. The sauce works well with roasted vegetables too.
