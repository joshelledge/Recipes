Dijon Sauce for Smashed Potatoes

Description: A creamy Dijon sauce perfect for drizzling over smashed potatoes, adding tang and depth of flavor.

Ingredients

    2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

    Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat.
  1. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes
  2. stirring constantly
  3. to create a roux.
  4. Gradually add milk
  5. whisking to combine
  6. and cook until the sauce thickens
  7. about 3-4 minutes.
  8. Stir in Dijon mustard
  9. white wine vinegar
  10. and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Remove from heat and serve warm over smashed potatoes. Garnish with parsley
  12. if desired.
