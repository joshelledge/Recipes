Dijon Sauce for Smashed Potatoes

Description: A tangy and creamy Dijon sauce that pairs perfectly with smashed or roasted potatoes for an elevated side dish.

Ingredients

    1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp whole grain mustard (optional)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

    In a small saucepan
  1. heat the butter over medium-low heat until melted.
  2. Stir in the heavy cream
  3. Dijon mustard
  4. and whole grain mustard
  5. if using.
  6. Add the honey and garlic powder
  7. whisking to combine.
  8. Simmer gently for 2-3 minutes
  9. stirring frequently
  10. until the sauce thickens slightly.
  11. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  12. Drizzle the sauce over smashed potatoes and serve immediately.
