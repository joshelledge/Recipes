Stuffing with Sausage and Cranberries

Description: A hearty and flavorful stuffing made with savory sausage, sweet cranberries, and aromatic herbs, perfect for your holiday table.

Ingredients

    1 lb sausage (crumbled)
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 2 bags (12 oz each) pre-seasoned stuffing mix
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 medium onion
  • chopped
  • 2 celery stalks
  • diced
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley
  • chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

    Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  1. In a large skillet
  2. cook sausage over medium heat until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet
  4. melt butter and sauté onion and celery until softened
  5. about 5 minutes.
  6. In a large mixing bowl
  7. combine stuffing mix
  8. cooked sausage
  9. sautéed vegetables
  10. cranberries
  11. and parsley.
  12. Gradually stir in chicken broth until the stuffing is moist but not soggy.
  13. Season with salt and pepper
  14. then transfer to a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  15. Bake uncovered for 35-45 minutes
  16. or until the top is golden and crispy.
