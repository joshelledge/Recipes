Cranberry Sauce with Apple Mango Twist

Description: A unique cranberry sauce with apple mango juice, whole cranberries, and fresh apples for a festive holiday twist.

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces fresh or frozen cranberries
  • 1 cup apple mango juice
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 small Honeycrisp apples, peeled and diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • Optional: Fresh rosemary sprigs for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine cranberries, apple mango juice, and sugar. Stir to combine.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add diced apples, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Simmer for 10-12 minutes until cranberries burst and the sauce thickens.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in orange zest. Let cool to room temperature before serving.
  5. Optional: Garnish with fresh rosemary sprigs before serving for an aromatic touch.
