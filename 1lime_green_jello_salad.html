Lime Green Jell-O Salad

Description: A nostalgic and sweet lime Jell-O salad with cottage cheese, pineapple, and whipped topping for a delightful side or dessert.

Ingredients

    2 boxes (3 oz each) lime Jell-O mix
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 can (20 oz) crushed pineapple
  • drained
  • 1 cup whipped topping

Instructions

    In a large mixing bowl
  1. dissolve the lime Jell-O mix in boiling water.
  2. Stir in the cold water and mix well. Let the Jell-O cool to room temperature.
  3. Fold in the cottage cheese and drained crushed pineapple.
  4. Gently fold in the whipped topping until fully combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into a serving dish or mold and refrigerate for at least 4 hours
  6. or until set.
