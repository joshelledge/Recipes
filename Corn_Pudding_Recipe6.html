STK-Inspired Corn Pudding

Description: A creamy and savory corn pudding inspired by STK's rich and velvety recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal (fine or medium grind)
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cream (optional, for extra richness)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, for greasing

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C). Butter a baking dish and set aside.
  2. In a medium saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until just simmering. Gradually whisk in the cornmeal to prevent clumping.
  3. Reduce heat and cook, stirring frequently, until the mixture thickens, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir in the Parmesan cheese, sugar, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using). Adjust sweetness or spice to taste.
  5. Optional: For a creamier texture, stir in 1/2 cup of heavy cream before transferring to the baking dish.
  6. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and smooth the top. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden and set.
  7. Serve warm and garnish with a sprinkle of fresh herbs, if desired.
