STK-Inspired Braised Short Ribs

Description: Tender, fall-off-the-bone short ribs braised in a rich red wine sauce, flavored with aromatic herbs and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in beef short ribs (about 2 lbs)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 large carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups red wine
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (for finishing sauce)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C). Pat the short ribs dry and season generously with salt and black pepper.
  2. In a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat, heat olive oil. Sear the short ribs on all sides until deeply browned, about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove the ribs and set aside.
  3. Add the onion, carrots, and celery to the pot. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until softened. Stir in the garlic and tomato paste, and cook for another minute.
  4. Pour in the red wine and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. Simmer for 5 minutes to reduce slightly.
  5. Add the beef broth, thyme, and rosemary. Return the short ribs to the pot, submerging them in the liquid. Cover with a lid.
  6. Transfer the pot to the oven and braise for 3-4 hours, or until the meat is tender and falling off the bone.
  7. Remove the ribs from the pot and strain the sauce through a fine-mesh sieve. Discard the solids. Skim excess fat and finish the sauce with 2 tablespoons of butter.
  8. Serve the short ribs warm with the rich sauce spooned over the top.

Notes

For the best results, use a high-quality red wine and take your time searing the short ribs – the browning develops incredible flavor. The sauce can be made ahead and reheated for serving.
