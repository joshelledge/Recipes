Smash Burgers

Description: Juicy smash burgers with crispy caramelized edges, melty cheddar cheese, and pickles on a toasted brioche bun. The ultimate burger experience!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • 4 brioche burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon high-smoke point oil (e.g., canola or avocado oil)
  • 1/4 cup sliced dill pickles
  • 2 tablespoons butter (for toasting buns)
  • Optional toppings: ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, lettuce, tomato

Instructions

  1. Preheat a cast iron skillet or griddle over high heat until it’s smoking hot.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions (about 4 oz each) without overworking it. Keep the portions loose for a better texture.
  3. Lightly butter the brioche buns and toast them in the skillet until golden brown. Set aside.
  4. Add a small amount of oil to the skillet. Place a portion of ground beef onto the hot skillet. Using a heavy spatula or burger press, firmly smash the beef into a thin patty (about 1/4 inch thick).
  5. Season the patty with kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper. Let it cook undisturbed for 1-2 minutes until the edges are crispy and caramelized.
  6. Flip the patty and immediately place a slice of cheddar cheese on top. Cook for another 30-45 seconds until the cheese melts.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties onto the toasted buns. Add pickles and any other desired toppings such as ketchup, mustard, or mayo.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy the crispy, juicy smash burger experience!

Notes

To achieve the best crust, make sure your skillet or griddle is extremely hot before smashing the burgers. Use an 80/20 ground beef blend for the juiciest results. Toasting the buns with butter adds extra flavor and texture.
