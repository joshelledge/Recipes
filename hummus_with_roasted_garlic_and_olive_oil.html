Hummus with Roasted Garlic and Olive Oil

Description: A smooth and creamy hummus made with roasted garlic and drizzled with extra virgin olive oil for a rich and flavorful dip.

Ingredients

    2 cups canned chickpeas
  • peeled
  • 3 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp roasted garlic
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Ice water as needed
  • Olive oil and paprika for garnish

Instructions

    In a food processor
  1. combine chickpeas
  2. tahini
  3. roasted garlic
  4. lemon juice
  5. olive oil
  6. and cumin.
  7. Blend until smooth
  8. adding ice water a tablespoon at a time to achieve the desired consistency.
  9. Season with salt to taste and blend again briefly.
  10. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of paprika.
  11. Serve with pita bread
  12. fresh vegetables
  13. or crackers.
