Hummus with Roasted Garlic and Olive Oil

Description: A creamy homemade hummus recipe enhanced with roasted garlic and a drizzle of olive oil. Perfect for dipping or spreading!

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 3 cloves roasted garlic
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2-3 tablespoons cold water
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: Paprika and chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Wrap the garlic cloves in foil and roast for 30-40 minutes until soft and golden.
  2. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, roasted garlic, tahini, lemon juice, cumin, and salt.
  3. With the processor running, slowly drizzle in 3 tablespoons of olive oil and 2-3 tablespoons of cold water until smooth and creamy.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed, adding more salt, lemon juice, or water for desired consistency.
  5. Spoon the hummus into a bowl and drizzle with additional olive oil. Garnish with paprika and chopped parsley, if desired.
