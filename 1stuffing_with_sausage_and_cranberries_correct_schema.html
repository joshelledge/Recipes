Stuffing with Sausage and Cranberries

Description: A hearty Thanksgiving stuffing with sausage, cranberries, and aromatic herbs.

Ingredients

    1 bag (12 oz) stuffing mix
  • 1 lb sausage
  • cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup celery
  • diced
  • 1 medium onion
  • diced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • melted
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  5. celery

  7. Add chicken broth and melted butter
  8. stirring until evenly moistened.
  9. Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish and cover with foil.
