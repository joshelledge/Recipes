Stuffing with Sausage and Cranberries

Description: A flavorful Thanksgiving stuffing made with sausage, cranberries, and fresh herbs. Perfectly crispy on top and moist inside.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces ground sausage
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • 12 cups cubed bread (day-old or slightly stale)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cups chicken or turkey broth
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon fresh sage, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: 1/4 cup chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F and grease a large casserole dish.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the sausage over medium heat until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, melt butter and sauté the onion and celery until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread cubes, sausage, onion mixture, cranberries, and fresh herbs.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the broth and eggs. Pour over the bread mixture and gently toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Transfer the mixture to the prepared casserole dish and bake, uncovered, for 30-40 minutes or until the top is golden and crispy.
  7. Optional: Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.
