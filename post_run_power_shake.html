Post-Run Power Shake

Description: A creamy, indulgent protein shake perfect for rebuilding muscles and replenishing energy post-run. Packed with protein, carbs, and healthy fats.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop of Legion Whey Protein (vanilla flavor)
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 1/3 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter or almond butter
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds or hemp hearts
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk (or water)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla paste or extract
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • 1-2 ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and garnish with cocoa powder or chia seeds, if desired.

Notes

This shake balances protein, carbs, and fats to help you recover after a run. Customize it with optional cocoa powder or strawberries for extra flavor.
