Perfect Creamy Mashed Potatoes

Description: Smooth, buttery mashed potatoes made with Yukon Gold potatoes, cream, and butter. Achieve restaurant-quality texture using a potato ricer.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and diced into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Optional garnish: melted butter, chives, or parsley

Instructions

  1. Peel and dice the Yukon Gold potatoes into small, evenly sized cubes for even cooking.
  2. Place the potatoes in a large pot and cover with cold water. Add a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce to a simmer and cook for 15-20 minutes until tender.
  3. While the potatoes cook, heat the heavy cream, milk, and butter in a small saucepan over low heat until warm. Do not let it boil.
  4. Drain the potatoes and let them sit for 1-2 minutes to release excess moisture.
  5. Pass the potatoes through a potato ricer or food mill into a large bowl. This step ensures a perfectly smooth texture.
  6. Gently stir in the warm cream and butter mixture until fully incorporated. Season with kosher salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with melted butter, chives, or parsley if desired.

Notes

To achieve the smoothest mashed potatoes, use a potato ricer or food mill instead of mashing by hand. Yukon Gold potatoes provide the perfect creamy texture and buttery flavor. Heating the cream and butter prevents the potatoes from becoming gummy.
