Hummus with Roasted Garlic and Olive Oil

Description: A smooth and creamy hummus recipe featuring the rich flavor of roasted garlic and a drizzle of olive oil.

Ingredients

    2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • skins removed
  • 3 tbsp tahini
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil (plus extra for garnish)
  • 1/4 cup water (as needed)
  • 3 cloves roasted garlic
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Paprika and chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

    Add chickpeas
  1. tahini
  2. lemon juice
  3. olive oil
  4. roasted garlic
  5. cumin
  6. and salt to a food processor.
  7. Blend until smooth
  8. adding water a little at a time to reach your desired consistency.
  9. Taste and adjust seasoning with additional salt or lemon juice
  10. if needed.
  11. Transfer the hummus to a serving dish and drizzle with olive oil. Garnish with paprika and parsley
  12. if desired.
