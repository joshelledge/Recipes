Herb-Butter Roast Chicken

Description: A perfectly roasted chicken with a crispy, golden skin basted in a savory herb butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (4-5 pounds)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh sage, chopped
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425째F and pat the chicken dry with paper towels.
  2. In a bowl, mix the softened butter with garlic, rosemary, thyme, sage, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Rub the herb butter all over the chicken, including under the skin. Stuff the cavity with the halved lemon.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the chicken for extra crispiness.
  5. Place the chicken breast-side up in a roasting pan or on a rack in a baking dish.
  6. Roast in the preheated oven for 1 hour 20 minutes to 1 hour 30 minutes, basting occasionally with the pan juices.
  7. Remove from the oven when the internal temperature reaches 165째F. Let the chicken rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired, and serve with roasted vegetables or potatoes.

Notes

For a richer flavor, baste the chicken every 20 minutes with the melted butter and pan juices. Use a meat thermometer to ensure the chicken is fully cooked.
