Herb-Butter Roast Chicken

Description: A juicy and flavorful roast chicken seasoned with a blend of fresh herbs and butter for a perfect main course.

Ingredients

    1 whole chicken (4-5 lbs)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • softened
  • 2 tbsp fresh rosemary
  • chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh thyme
  • chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley
  • chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • minced
  • 1 lemon
  • halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

    Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  1. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels and season the cavity with salt and pepper.
  2. In a small bowl
  3. mix softened butter
  4. rosemary
  5. thyme
  6. parsley
  7. and garlic until combined.
  8. Gently loosen the skin of the chicken and spread the herb butter underneath the skin and on top of the chicken.
  9. Place lemon halves inside the cavity of the chicken.
  10. Truss the chicken and place it on a roasting rack in a roasting pan.
  11. Roast the chicken for 1 1/2 hours
  12. or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) in the thickest part of the thigh.
  13. Let the chicken rest for 10-15 minutes before carving.
