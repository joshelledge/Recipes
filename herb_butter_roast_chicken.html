Herb-Butter Roast Chicken

Description: A succulent roast chicken coated in herb-infused butter, delivering crisp golden skin and juicy, flavorful meat.

Ingredients

    1 whole chicken (4-5 lbs)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • softened
  • 2 tbsp fresh rosemary
  • minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh thyme
  • minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley
  • minced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • minced
  • 1 lemon
  • halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 onion
  • quartered
  • 2 cups chicken broth

Instructions

    Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  1. In a bowl
  2. mix softened butter with rosemary
  3. thyme
  4. parsley
  5. garlic
  6. salt
  7. and pepper.
  8. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Gently loosen the skin over the breast and thighs
  9. and spread the herb butter under the skin.
  10. Rub any remaining butter over the outside of the chicken.
  11. Stuff the cavity with the halved lemon and onion quarters.
  12. Place the chicken in a roasting pan
  13. breast side up
  14. and pour chicken broth into the pan.
  15. Roast for 1 hour 30 minutes
  16. basting every 30 minutes
  17. or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C) in the thickest part of the breast.
  18. Let rest for 10-15 minutes before carving and serving.
