STK-Inspired Corn Pudding

Author: Josh Elledge

Description: A creamy and savory corn pudding inspired by the beloved STK recipe. Perfectly baked with rich cream, Parmesan, and cornmeal for a luxurious side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal (fine or medium grind)
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen corn kernels (thawed if frozen)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chives, finely chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a cast iron skillet or baking dish with butter.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the cornmeal, milk, and heavy cream. Cook over medium heat, whisking constantly, until the mixture thickens (5-7 minutes). Remove from heat.
  3. Stir in the melted butter, sugar, salt, and black pepper. Let it cool slightly, then whisk in the beaten eggs.
  4. Fold in the corn kernels and grated Parmesan cheese until evenly mixed.
  5. Pour the mixture into the prepared skillet or baking dish. Smooth the top with a spatula and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the center is set.
  6. Sprinkle with fresh chives before serving warm.

Total Time: 50 minutes | Servings: 6
