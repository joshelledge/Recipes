STK-Inspired Corn Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cornmeal (fine or medium grind)
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen corn kernels (thawed if frozen)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chives, finely chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a cast iron skillet or baking dish with butter.
  2. Cook the Cornmeal Base: In a medium saucepan, combine the cornmeal, milk, and heavy cream. Cook over medium heat, whisking constantly, until the mixture thickens (5-7 minutes). Remove from heat.
  3. Incorporate Butter and Eggs: Stir in the melted butter, sugar, salt, and black pepper. Let cool slightly, then whisk in the eggs.
  4. Add Corn and Cheese: Fold in the corn kernels and grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Assemble and Bake: Pour the mixture into the prepared skillet or baking dish. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until edges are golden and center is set.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle with fresh chives and serve warm.

Optional Upgrades:

  • Drizzle with honey butter for a sweet-savory touch.
  • Add crumbled bacon for extra flavor.
  • Finish with a drizzle of truffle oil for luxury.
