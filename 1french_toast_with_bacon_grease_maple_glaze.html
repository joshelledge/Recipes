French Toast with Bacon Grease Maple Glaze

Description: Rich and indulgent French toast cooked in bacon grease and finished with a maple syrup glaze.

Ingredients

    6 slices of brioche or challah bread
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3 tbsp bacon grease
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • Butter for serving

Instructions

    Whisk together eggs
  1. milk
  2. vanilla
  3. and cinnamon in a large bowl.
  4. Heat bacon grease in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture
  6. ensuring it is fully coated.
  7. Cook the bread slices in the skillet until golden brown on each side
  8. about 2-3 minutes per side.
  9. Drizzle with warm maple syrup and serve with butter.
