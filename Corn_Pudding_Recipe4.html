STK-Inspired Corn Pudding

A creamy and savory corn pudding inspired by the famous STK restaurant.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal (fine or medium grind)
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a saucepan, heat milk over medium heat until warm but not boiling.
  3. Slowly whisk in the cornmeal until smooth and thickened.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese, sugar, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish.
  6. Bake for 35–40 minutes or until set and golden on top.

Nutrition

Calories: 320 | Fat: 18g | Carbs: 32g | Protein: 7g

Notes

This recipe replicates the creamy and smooth texture of STK's corn pudding. Make sure to whisk the cornmeal into warm milk gradually to avoid lumps. Serve immediately for the best texture. Perfect as a holiday side dish!
