French-Style Scrambled Eggs

Description: Soft and creamy scrambled eggs cooked gently to achieve a luxurious texture, perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

    4 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream or whole milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives or parsley (optional)

Instructions

    Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk gently until the yolks and whites are just combined.
  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over low heat and add the butter
  2. allowing it to melt slowly.
  3. Pour the eggs into the skillet and stir continuously with a spatula
  4. scraping the bottom to create small curds.
  5. Cook gently
  6. stirring constantly
  7. until the eggs are creamy and slightly runny (they will continue to cook off the heat).
  8. Remove from heat and stir in heavy cream or milk for added creaminess.
  9. Season with salt and pepper and garnish with chopped chives or parsley
  10. if desired.
