French-Style Scrambled Eggs

Description: Delicate, creamy, and custardy scrambled eggs cooked low and slow in the French style. Perfect for a luxurious breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons whole milk or cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Pinch of freshly ground black pepper
  • Optional: Fresh chives or parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add milk or cream, and whisk until the yolks and whites are completely combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over low heat and melt the butter, ensuring it does not brown.
  3. Pour the eggs into the skillet and cook over low heat, stirring continuously with a spatula or wooden spoon.
  4. Keep stirring gently and consistently, scraping the bottom of the skillet to prevent the eggs from setting too quickly.
  5. Once the eggs are soft, creamy, and slightly runny, remove the skillet from heat (they will continue cooking from residual heat).
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh chives or parsley if desired. Serve immediately.
