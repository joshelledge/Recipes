Butternut Squash Soup with Disney-Inspired Spices

Description: A rich and creamy butternut squash soup enhanced with warm spices inspired by Disney's Animal Kingdom Lodge.

Ingredients

    1 large butternut squash
  • peeled and cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • minced
  • 2 small apples
  • peeled and chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk or heavy cream
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp allspice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pumpkin seeds or croutons for garnish

Instructions

    Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic
  1. and sauté until softened.
  2. Stir in butternut squash and apples. Cook for 5 minutes
  3. stirring occasionally.
  4. Add broth
  5. cinnamon
  6. nutmeg
  7. and allspice. Bring to a boil
  8. then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes
  9. until squash is tender.
  10. Use an immersion blender or transfer to a blender to puree until smooth.
  11. Stir in coconut milk or cream and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  12. Serve hot
  13. garnished with pumpkin seeds or croutons.
