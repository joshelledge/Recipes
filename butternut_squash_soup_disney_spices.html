Butternut Squash Soup with Disney-Inspired Spices

Description: A creamy, warm butternut squash soup infused with Disney-inspired spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger. Perfect for fall and cozy dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 large butternut squash (about 3 pounds), peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: Fresh thyme or parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until soft, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the cubed butternut squash to the pot and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the squash is tender.
  4. Carefully blend the soup using an immersion blender or transfer it to a blender in batches until smooth and creamy.
  5. Return the soup to the pot, stir in the heavy cream, cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Simmer for 5 more minutes to meld the flavors. Serve hot, garnished with fresh thyme or parsley if desired.

Notes

For a dairy-free version, substitute the heavy cream with coconut milk. Add a pinch of cayenne pepper for a subtle kick.
