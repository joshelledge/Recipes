French Toast with Bacon Grease Maple Glaze

Description: A decadent French toast recipe cooked in bacon grease and topped with a rich maple glaze for the ultimate indulgence.

Ingredients

    8 slices thick-cut brioche bread
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup bacon grease
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

    In a shallow bowl
  1. whisk together eggs
  2. milk
  3. vanilla extract
  4. and cinnamon.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of bacon grease.
  6. Dip each slice of brioche bread into the egg mixture
  7. ensuring both sides are coated.
  8. Cook each slice in the skillet for 2-3 minutes per side
  9. or until golden brown.
  10. In a small saucepan
  11. heat maple syrup
  12. butter
  13. and a pinch of salt until warm and combined.
  14. Serve the French toast topped with the warm maple glaze.
