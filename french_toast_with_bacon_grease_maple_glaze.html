French Toast with Bacon Grease Maple Glaze

Description: A decadent twist on classic French toast, this recipe uses bacon grease for a rich, savory glaze combined with maple syrup for the perfect sweet-and-salty flavor.

Ingredients

    8 slices French bread (thick-cut)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 tbsp bacon grease (reserved from cooked bacon)
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 6-8 slices cooked bacon
  • Butter for cooking

Instructions

    In a large bowl
  1. whisk together eggs
  2. milk
  3. vanilla extract
  4. cinnamon
  5. and salt until well combined.
  6. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat and add a small amount of butter to coat.
  7. Dip each slice of French bread into the egg mixture
  8. allowing it to soak for about 10-15 seconds per side.
  9. Cook the soaked bread slices on the skillet until golden brown on both sides
  10. about 3-4 minutes per side.
  11. While the French toast cooks
  12. combine reserved bacon grease and maple syrup in a small saucepan over low heat. Stir until smooth and warmed.
  13. Serve French toast topped with the bacon grease maple glaze and crispy bacon slices.
