French Toast with Bacon Grease Maple Glaze

Description: Decadent French toast cooked in bacon grease and drizzled with a rich maple glaze for the ultimate sweet and savory breakfast treat.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices thick French bread, about 1 inch thick
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup bacon grease
  • 1/2 cup pure maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Optional: Powdered sugar and fresh fruit for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, and sugar until well combined.
  2. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium heat and add bacon grease.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are coated but not overly soaked.
  4. Cook the bread slices on the skillet for 2-3 minutes per side, until golden brown and crispy.
  5. In a small saucepan, combine maple syrup and butter. Heat over low heat, stirring until butter melts and glaze is warm.
  6. Serve French toast with warm maple glaze drizzled on top. Garnish with powdered sugar or fresh fruit if desired.
