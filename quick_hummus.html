Quick Hummus Recipe

Description: A creamy and smooth hummus recipe perfect for dipping or spreading. Includes optional toppings like paprika, olive oil, or za’atar.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional toppings: paprika, olive oil drizzle, za’atar, chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Place chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and cumin into a food processor.
  2. Blend until smooth, adding water a tablespoon at a time to achieve desired consistency.
  3. Season with salt to taste.
  4. Transfer to a serving bowl and garnish with optional toppings.
