STK-Inspired Corn Pudding

Author: Josh Elledge

Description: A creamy and savory corn pudding inspired by the famous STK restaurant.

Image

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal (fine or medium grind)
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/2 cups corn kernels (fresh or thawed frozen)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chives, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a cast iron skillet or baking dish with butter.
  2. In a saucepan, combine cornmeal, milk, and heavy cream. Whisk constantly over medium heat until thickened (5-7 minutes).
  3. Remove from heat. Stir in melted butter, sugar, salt, and pepper. Let cool slightly, then whisk in the eggs.
  4. Fold in corn kernels and Parmesan cheese until well combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into the prepared skillet. Smooth the top and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the center is set.
  6. Sprinkle with fresh chives before serving warm.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 320 | Fat: 18g | Carbs: 32g | Protein: 7g

Notes

Whisk constantly while cooking the cornmeal to avoid lumps. For the best results, use a cast iron skillet for crispy edges. Serve warm for the creamiest texture.
