Lime Green Jell-O Salad

Description: A nostalgic and creamy lime Jell-O salad, perfect as a dessert or a sweet side dish for family gatherings.

Ingredients

    2 packages (3 oz each) lime Jell-O
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple (drained)
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 cup whipped cream or Cool Whip
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Instructions

    In a large bowl
  1. dissolve the lime Jell-O in boiling water
  2. stirring until completely dissolved.
  3. Add cold water and mix well. Allow to cool to room temperature.
  4. Stir in the drained pineapple
  5. cottage cheese
  6. and whipped cream until fully combined.
  7. If using
  8. fold in the chopped nuts.
  9. Pour the mixture into a Jell-O mold or serving dish and refrigerate for at least 4 hours
  10. or until set.
  11. Unmold the Jell-O salad onto a plate before serving
  12. if desired.
