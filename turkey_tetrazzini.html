Turkey Tetrazzini

Description: A creamy and indulgent Turkey Tetrazzini made with leftover turkey, pasta, a mix of Alfredo and pesto sauce, and baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked turkey, shredded
  • 12 oz spaghetti or fettuccine
  • 1 cup Alfredo sauce
  • 1/2 cup pesto sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with butter.
  2. Cook the pasta until al dente according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, combine Alfredo sauce, pesto sauce, chicken broth, garlic powder, black pepper, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Mix in the cooked turkey and pasta until evenly coated.
  5. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and top with breadcrumbs, Parmesan, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let cool slightly before serving. Garnish with parsley if desired.
