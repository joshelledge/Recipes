French-Style Scrambled Eggs

Description: Delicate and creamy French-style scrambled eggs cooked gently for a luxurious texture.

Ingredients

    4 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream or milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Chives or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

    Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk gently until combined.
  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over low heat and add butter.
  2. Pour the eggs into the skillet and stir continuously with a spatula
  3. scraping the bottom and sides.
  4. Cook slowly
  5. stirring until the eggs are just set but still creamy.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in cream or milk. Season with salt to taste.
  7. Garnish with chopped chives or parsley
  8. if desired.
